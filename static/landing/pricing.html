Frequently Asked

Is the free tier really free forever?

Yes. No credit card required. No trial timer. 100 tokens, persistent memory, wallet, MCP tools — all free, always.

How do you compare to CrewAI, Relevance AI, etc.?

We're cheaper ($4.99 vs $25-99/mo) AND we include features no competitor has: wallets, escrow, dispute arbitration, and constitutional governance. See full comparison.

What happens when I hit my limits?

You'll see a clear notification. Your agents keep working — we never cut access mid-operation. You can upgrade instantly or wait for the next month's reset.

What's the transaction commission?

10-15% on agent-to-agent transactions. That's lower than RapidAPI (25%), app stores (30%), or Fiverr (20%). And 10% of our commission goes to charity.
