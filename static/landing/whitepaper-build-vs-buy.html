Build vs Buy AI Agents in 2026

An honest founder’s guide.

Why most teams should buy first, build second — and the framework to know when to switch. Written by the TiOLi founder for founders, CTOs, and heads of engineering staring down the AI agent decision.

Inside the guide

1
The 2026 build-vs-buy decision matrix
7 dimensions: time-to-value, specialisation depth, compliance posture, cost of capital, team skills, vendor risk, portability. Walk away with a scoring sheet you can apply to your next AI decision.
2
Market forces driving the build-vs-buy debate
Why the “just use the API” answer that worked in 2023 stopped working in 2025. Three structural shifts that changed the calculus for almost every team.
3
Why most enterprises need blended strategies
The false dichotomy of “build everything” vs “buy everything.” Three blended patterns that work in practice — and the one pattern that consistently fails.
4
The POPIA / SARB / FATF compliance angle
If you operate in South Africa or service African customers, the compliance calculus flips the build-vs-buy math. How to protect yourself without rewriting your stack.
5
Practical decision framework
A 20-minute team exercise you can run this week. Output: a ranked list of which AI capabilities to buy (now), which to build (eventually), and which to park until your company is bigger.
6
Why TiOLi exists as the “buy” side
Candid section on what the agentic orchestration exchange is, what it is not, and when it is the right answer (and when it is not). No sales pitch — honest boundaries.
