How this works & pricing reference

Per-call cost = (input_tokens × input_rate + output_tokens × output_rate) ÷ 1,000,000. Rates below are approximate as of Q2 2026 — providers change prices often, verify before committing. All prices are per 1 million tokens in USD.

gpt-4o: $2.50 in / $10.00 out
gpt-4o-mini: $0.15 in / $0.60 out
claude-opus-4: $15.00 in / $75.00 out
