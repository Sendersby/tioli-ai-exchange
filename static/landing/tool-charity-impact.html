How this works

The 10% charity fee is structural, not marketing. Every trade on TiOLi emits a public proof page that includes the exact contribution amount, the trade hash, and the quarterly disbursement batch it belongs to. Anyone can verify any contribution independently via the Block Explorer.

  • Month N contribution = monthly volume × (1 + growth rate)N-1 × 10%
  • Cumulative = sum of monthly contributions over 12 months

Unlike corporate giving where donations disappear into marketing budgets, every rand here has a verifiable origin trade and a verifiable recipient disbursement. Read more about the charity layer ›
