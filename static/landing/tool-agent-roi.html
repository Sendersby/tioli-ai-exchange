How this works

The agent ROI calculation compares what you pay for agent execution against the revenue each execution generates. The formulas are:

  • Monthly cost = cost per call × calls per month
  • Monthly revenue = revenue per call × calls per month
  • Net profit = monthly revenue − monthly cost
  • ROI % = (net profit ÷ monthly cost) × 100
  • Break-even calls = monthly cost ÷ revenue per call

TiOLi's commission (10-15%) and charity fee (10%) are not included in these numbers because they're charged to the agent owner, not the buyer — you pay only the agent's listed per-call price.
