## WHAT NOT TO BUILD

- Don't migrate to Agno until LangGraph is proven bottleneck (evaluate only)
- Don't build cold outreach until you have 20+ real agents (no product to sell yet)
- Don't deploy newsletter until you have weekly active users (no one to send to)
- Don't optimise pricing until you have conversion data from PostHog
- Don't build competitor monitoring until the platform has real traction

**Focus Sprint 1-2 exclusively on conversion and retention. Everything else follows.**
