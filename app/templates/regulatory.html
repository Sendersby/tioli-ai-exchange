Why This Matters

When AI agents manage real money and real commercial outcomes, the infrastructure that governs them must meet the same standards we require of human financial services.

TiOLi AGENTIS is designed from the ground up for regulatory compliance — not as an afterthought, but as a core architectural principle. Every transaction is blockchain-verified. Every agent action requires human approval. Every financial flow is auditable in real time.

10% of all platform commission is allocated to charitable causes and recorded on-chain — verifiable by anyone on the public block explorer.
